AI Transparency Statement

🤖 How Artificial Intelligence Was Used in This Assessment

This website was created with the assistance of Claude (Claude.ai), an AI tool developed by Anthropic. In accordance with PIA's academic integrity policy and the ECE502 unit guidelines (which permit the use of GenAI), I am disclosing how AI was used in the creation of this assessment.

AI was used to:

  • Help draft and refine written content for each page, based on ECE502 unit literature and lecture materials provided in the course
  • Suggest accessible and family-friendly language — rephrasing academic content into plain English suitable for a family audience
  • Help plan the structure and logical navigation of the website pages
  • Build the HTML and CSS code for the website layout and design
  • Support organisation of the APA7 reference list

How academic integrity was maintained:

  • All academic content and ideas were drawn from ECE502 unit literature, lecture slides and prescribed readings — not from AI-generated knowledge
  • All references were drawn directly from unit materials and verified against original sources
  • Final decisions about content accuracy, structure, presentation and appropriateness were made by the student
  • AI was used as a drafting and planning tool — not as a substitute for academic understanding or critical thinking
